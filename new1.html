Finance And Loan Services


overview

A loan application is an entire process starting from the negotiation of terms until the loan is reviewed and approved for disbursement. A loan application form is first provided to the client, and the client is required to fill it out correctly before the application form proceeds to the review stage. The client must provide truthful information, including their personal details, physical address, financial statements, evidence of asset ownership, etc. The information should be verifiable, and the credit officer should verify the accuracy and truthfulness of the information provided in the application document.
